Upgrades

Walls LVL LVL 1 Bigger walls, so escape rate decreases by 10%, but maintenance cost of $10 per day per level.
Luxury LVL LVL 1 Luxurious items, prisoners will like you so riot rate decreases(2%), happiness increases(5%), but increases money usage(+100/lvl)!
Guards LVL LVL 1 More guards! More guards means less escape rate(-2%) but more money usage($150/day/guard), and also prevents additional escape rate from prisoners. (Adds one guard per level. Additional expense per day will be present!)
Tax Accountant LVL LVL 1 File for tax reduction to the federal government. Reduces 1 tax visit per level, but adds a $50/level/day expense.
Prison capacity LVL LVL 1 Increases max amount of prisoners by 5 per level. Reduces income by $500 every level.

Welcome to Prison Simulator. In this game, your primary goal is to make money while keeping your prisoners secure by purchasing the upgrades. The game is capable of mobile use, although we personally think it's more beautiful on desktop. Your statistics are in the middle black bar, upgrades on the left, and the controls on the right.

Here's what you should do first:

  • Get as many guards as possible
  • Get as many prisoners as possible without getting over the amount of the guards.
  • Invest in upgrades!
  • Shakedown often!
  • Get the "tax accountant" upgrade to not get taxed!
